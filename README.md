##  Key Insights

• Some ads achieved very high impressions but low conversions.
• Engagement rates varied widely across campaigns.
• High-performing ads delivered strong CTR at lower cost.
• Several campaigns used high spend without matching results.
• Conversion efficiency differed significantly between ad types.

##  Recommendations

• Increase budget on top-performing ads.
• Reduce or stop ads with low CTR and weak conversions.
• Improve ad messaging and creative content.
• Test new audiences to improve engagement.
• Optimize bidding strategy to lower CPC

##  Conclusion

The analysis revealed clear performance differences across campaigns.
The dashboard makes it easier to identify what works and what needs improvement.
Overall, the project demonstrates how data-driven insights can guide better marketing decisions and improve future campaign outcomes.
